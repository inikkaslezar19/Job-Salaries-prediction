About Our Model

Our job salary prediction system uses multiple regression analysis, a statistical technique that examines the linear relationship between a dependent variable (salary) and multiple independent variables (experience, education, industry, etc.).

The model was trained on a comprehensive dataset of job salaries across various industries, locations, and experience levels. It considers multiple factors simultaneously to provide more accurate predictions than simple linear models.

Key factors considered in our model:

  • Years of professional experience
  • Educational attainment
  • Industry sector
  • Geographic location
  • Company size
  • Job role and seniority

While our model provides valuable insights, salary negotiations are complex and influenced by many factors not captured here.
